About Me

Hi! I'm chethan, a dedicated web developer and educator who believes that coding is a tool everyone should have the opportunity to learn. With several years of experience in web development, I specialize in creating dynamic and responsive websites, working with modern technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. Through my work and lessons, I strive to foster a learning environment where students can thrive and turn their curiosity into mastery.
